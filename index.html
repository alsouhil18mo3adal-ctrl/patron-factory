About Us · من نحن

From fabric to finished product — a complete expertise.

Based in Annaba, Patron Factory manages the entire lifecycle of clothing: from sewing and manufacturing to branding (private label), and direct delivery across Algeria.

Our workshop masters both traditional Algerian attire — burnous, haïk, djellaba, caftan, karakou — and modern ready-to-wear. This dual expertise allows us to serve all market segments with the same artisanal rigor and industrial capacity.

The Process · خطوات الإنتاج

From order to delivery.

A rigorous four-step process to guarantee quality, deadlines and total satisfaction.

01

Design Study & Pattern Making

Model definition, material selection and pattern adjustment according to your technical specifications.

02

Manufacturing Annaba Workshop

Digital cutting, precision sewing, embroidery and ornaments, finishing and rigorous quality control in-house.

03

Branding Label & Tagging

Application of your label and personalized packaging according to your private label standards.

04

Delivery All Wilayas

Direct shipping to your wilaya: secure packaging and tracking notification until receipt.
